About Arsenal Football Club

Pride of North London — Est. 1886

Our History

Arsenal Football Club was founded in 1886 in Woolwich, South London, before moving to Highbury and later the iconic Emirates Stadium. Known for attractive football, passionate fans, and legendary players, Arsenal remains one of the most successful clubs in English history.

Our Mission

To inspire fans and communities through world-class football, innovation, and respect — both on and off the pitch.

Our Vision

To be recognized globally as a leading football club that values teamwork, development, and community spirit.
